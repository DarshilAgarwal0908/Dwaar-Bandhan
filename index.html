About Us

At Dwaar Bandhan, we celebrate the timeless beauty of Indian tradition through handcrafted decorative creations designed to bring warmth, culture, and elegance into every home. Our products, ranging from traditional hangings to thoughtful gifting pieces, are inspired by the rich heritage of India and crafted with love and attention to detail.

Dwaar Bandhan was founded by Manisha Jindal and Puman Jindal, two passionate women from the small village of Chirkunda in Dhanbad, Jharkhand. In a place where opportunities for women to work and grow are often limited, they chose to follow their dream with courage and determination. With the unwavering support of their family, they turned their vision into a growing venture that not only reflects creativity but also empowers women to believe in their potential.

Our journey is not just about creating beautiful products—it is about breaking barriers, inspiring change, and building a future where tradition and progress go hand in hand. Every piece we design carries a story of dedication, culture, and the spirit of women who dare to dream.

Supporting them in this journey is Darshil Agarwal, who is committed to helping the brand expand and reach new heights while staying true to its roots.

At Dwaar Bandhan, we aim to transform spaces into expressions of tradition and emotion, making every decoration more than just an ornament—it becomes a meaningful connection to our heritage.

“Binding Traditions to Every Door.”
